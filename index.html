Ludo Rules

  • Roll the dice to move your pieces forward
  • Each player has 4 pieces of their color
  • Roll a 6 to bring a piece out from home
  • Roll a 6 to get another turn
  • Capture opponent's pieces by landing on the same square
  • First player to get all 4 pieces to home wins!
  • Each player must roll the dice to determine move
  • Pieces move in a specific path around the board

Player Red Wins!
